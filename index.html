About me

I'm interested & skilled in

This is Mehedi Hasan, an accomplished research enthusiast and development professional with a proven track record of impactful work across diverse roles in program management and community engagement. I hold a B.Sc. in Environmental Science from Jashore University of Science & Technology.

With a blend of technical expertise and strong interpersonal skills, I excel in facilitating strategic partnerships, conducting in-depth data-driven participatory research, and implementing community-based projects. My career spans various esteemed organizations, including Oxfam, the Centre for Environment and Climate Change Research (CECCR), and the Institute of Epidemiology Disease Control and Research (IEDCR).

I bring a unique combination of GIS and remote sensing proficiency, data analysis capabilities and hands-on project management experience. Whether it's utilizing drone for real-time remote sensing or engaging with local communities to drive sustainable development, my approach is adaptive, innovative, and collaborative.

I am dedicated to making a meaningful impact in the fields of development through research, driven by a passion for continuous learning and a commitment to fostering growth and resilience within communities. I thrive in dynamic environments, consistently delivering high-quality results under pressure and I am eager to contribute my skills and Experience to new challenges and opportunities.
